Frequently Asked Questions
What does the process cost?

Patient costs will vary depending on the prescription and the types of lenses ordered. Because each patient's order is customized, contact us for a consultation.

What types of frames can be relensed?

Patients are able to place new lenses into eyeglasses or Rx-able sunglasses.

What if the frames are very old?

Patients with older frames will need to have the frame evaluated in order to determine if that frame is stable enough for our technicians to work on. We suggest sending a picture or a video to our email prior to shipping your eyewear.

Can you fix scratched lenses?

Unfortunately, scratched lenses are unable to be repaired. Relense would be happy to order a new pair of lenses.

Can I still purchase lenses from Relense if I do not have a physical copy of my prescription?

Please contact us and we weill be happy to discuss options.
